MOST POWERFUL GUY IN THE FEED IS :

📝 Basic Info Name: Zenitsu Agatsuma (我妻 善逸) Age: 16 (at the start of the series) Affiliation: Demon Slayer Corps Rank: Demon Slayer (Kanoe → higher ranks as story progresses) Personality: Cowardly, anxious, emotional but kind-hearted and loyal ⚔️ Fighting Style Breathing Technique: Thunder Breathing (Kaminari no Kokyū) Forms: Officially learned all six forms but usually uses First Form: Thunderclap and Flash (Ichi no kata: Hekireki Issen). He later develops Seventh Form: Honoikazuchi no Kami, which he created himself. Strength: Extremely fast and powerful in his unconscious state Exceptional hearing (can sense demons and emotions through sound)
